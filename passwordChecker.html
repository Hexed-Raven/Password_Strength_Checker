🛡️ Good Practices for Password Hygiene

  • 🔑 Use a mix of uppercase, lowercase, numbers, and special characters
  • 🧠 Avoid dictionary words or anything easily guessable
  • 🔄 Don’t reuse the same password across multiple sites
  • 💼 Use a trusted password manager (e.g., Bitwarden, 1Password)
  • 🚫 Never share your password with anyone
  • 📵 Don’t store passwords in insecure places (e.g., Notes app, Excel files)
  • 🔐 Use Two-Factor Authentication (2FA) whenever possible
  • 🏷️ Follow the Zero Trust rule: trust no one, always verify
  • 🌐 Change your passwords regularly, especially after breaches
